- [ ] What are PropTypes used for? Please describe why it's important to type check our data in JavaScript.

PropTypes are used to check the types of properties passed to components and give us warnings if those types don't match. It's important not to mismatch types because many functions work with specific variable/property types.

- [ ] Describe a life-cycle event in React?

Lifecycle methods like '...DidMount' and '...WillUnmount' are used to manage state on components. They act as a kind of life/death cycle to avoid continuously rendering or re-rendering components, state and the like. 

- [ ] Explain the details of a Higher Order Component?

HOC's are componenets that take in components as arguments. This allows extended functionality to components that may share some similar data or functionality.
